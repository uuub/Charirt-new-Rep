Rosemary Caputo Solomon - Outreach Director

Is a Licensed Master of Social Work (LMSW), was born and raised in Brooklyn, NY. She is married and lives with her two children, one granddaughter and two dogs in Stony Brook, NY. Rosemary received her BA in English Literature with a minor in Education from Pace University in 1973. Rosemary became an English teacher and later chairperson of the English Dept for a business school in NYC. Later, she attended Stony Brook University from which she graduated with a Master of Social Work (MSW) degree in 1992. Rosemary then worked with various populations as a Social Worker and Therapist. These populations include pregnant and parenting teenagers, the homeless, people suffering from AIDS. She did therapy with people having various mental health issues as well as adoption concerns and issues. Rosemary did groups on depression, angels, women's issues and parent's issues regarding children. Among her many accomplishments Rosemary co-authored a children's book: Home Is Where You Hang Your Heart, about homelessness.
